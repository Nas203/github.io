WHO I AM

I have been writing scripts in Roblox for around 4 years now, and during this period, I have managed to become quite proficient at creating functional systems within my games.

By functional systems, I mean things such as economies, DataStore saving systems, teams and roles, tax systems, salary loops – anything that can add realism and depth to a game. On top of being equally skilled on the server side and the client side, I know how to implement RemoteEvents and RemoteFunctions effectively.

Another part of game development that I love is creating GUIs through coding. Things like health bars, hunger bars, sidebar shops, pop-up messages, and even welcome screens. Every single thing that I create in a game is custom-coded and does not rely on any third-party plugins.
